# Layer 6 Capability Catalog

## Purpose

This catalog defines the business growth capabilities Beeooking should add after the product and platform layers are established.

## Package Catalog

Recommended package structure:

- Foundation: Layer 1 club operating system.
- Development: Layer 1 plus Layer 2.
- Competition: Layer 1 plus Layer 3.
- Growth: Layers 1-4 with AI recommendations.
- Enterprise: Layers 1-5 with organizations, integrations, exports, analytics, and governance.

Potential add-ons:

- Extra club/location.
- Advanced analytics.
- AI recommendations.
- ClubLocker integration.
- Data warehouse export.
- Premium onboarding.
- Priority support.

## Sales Pipeline Stages

Recommended stages:

- Lead.
- Qualified.
- Demo scheduled.
- Demo completed.
- Proposal sent.
- Trial active.
- Negotiation.
- Closed won.
- Closed lost.

Recommended close-lost reasons:

- Price.
- Timing.
- Missing feature.
- Competitor selected.
- No decision.
- Not a fit.

## Onboarding Playbook

Recommended onboarding phases:

- Kickoff.
- Club profile setup.
- Facility and court setup.
- Staff and coach setup.
- Member import.
- Membership plan setup.
- Booking rules setup.
- Payment setup.
- Communications setup.
- Staff training.
- Test bookings/payments.
- Go-live.

## Customer Health Signals

Positive signals:

- Active bookings.
- Membership renewals.
- Program registrations.
- Staff logins.
- Coach usage.
- Parent/member engagement.
- AI recommendation conversion.

Risk signals:

- No admin logins.
- Low booking activity.
- Failed payments.
- Support escalation.
- Delayed onboarding.
- Declining attendance.
- Renewal approaching with low usage.
